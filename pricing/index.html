Pricing

Three tiers. One ladder.

ScoutLocal is always free for families. For businesses, each tier adds one clear capability: get listed, get found in the moment, then get prioritized with insight. Payment can support a profile — it never buys a badge, a hidden report, or family data.

Fair to families

Push limits apply on every tier — at most 1 push per business per day, 3 Beacon pushes per family per day, plus quiet hours. They protect families and aren't a paid feature. Featured's edge is unlimited go-lives, never more interruptions of any one family.

03 / the upgrade path

Move up the day it's worth it.

Free → Promote

From listed to live. You stop being a static entry and start broadcasting when you're open. The day you upgrade, everyone who already favorited you is invited to turn on alerts — your follower list starts warm, not at zero.

Promote → Featured

From live to prioritized. Unlimited go-lives for vendors who operate daily, top placement so you're seen first, and the analytics that tell you which deals and times actually move people — so you can do more of what works.

04 / military rate

A built-in rate for military-affiliated owners.

Verified military-affiliated owners pay the military-adjusted rate on paid tiers — $19/mo on Promote, with the Featured rate to be finalized. Eligibility and verification follow the Business Account Types rules.

Pricing status — draft. Free and Promote pricing is settled for launch. Featured is planned for after the August beta; its final price and military rate will be published before it goes live.
