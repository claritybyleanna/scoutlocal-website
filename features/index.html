Features

Belong faster after every move.

ScoutLocal is the trusted local guide for military families — the businesses and places worth knowing near your base, plus the military-spouse entrepreneurs worth supporting anywhere. Free for families. A home for the businesses who serve them.

Join the betaCreate your listing
For families · free, always

Land at a new base and know who to trust by the end of the week.

Every PCS means starting over: a new mechanic, a new salon, childcare you can actually trust, somewhere that already feels like yours. ScoutLocal hands you the shortlist the day you arrive — recommended by the families who already know.

Available now Free

  • Find trusted local businesses near your base, browsable by category
  • Discover military-spouse-owned businesses nationwide — and support your own community
  • Badges you can trust — Military Spouse Owned and Verified Local
  • See who's open right now — when a food truck or pop-up goes live, you'll know where and until when
  • Save your favorites so your list travels with you to the next base
  • Turn on alerts for the businesses you love, and get a heads-up the moment they're open
  • A weekly “What's Happening” email — new businesses, local spotlights, and tips for settling in
  • Free. Always. Families never pay.

Coming soon

  • Reviews from families like you — trust, in their own words
  • Local events in one place — MWR, ACS, and FRG happenings
  • More bases — ScoutLocal is rolling out base by base, and yours could be next
For businesses · free during beta

Get found by the military families around your base — the most loyal community you can earn.

Military families move often, spend locally, and stay with the businesses that take care of them. ScoutLocal puts you in front of them — and if your hours aren't fixed, it lets you tell them exactly when you're open and where.

Available now Free during beta

Your listing

  • A full profile: logo, photos, description, category, hours, contact, and links
  • A map pin families can navigate to

Get discovered

  • Show up in search and on the map
  • Earn a Verified Local or Military Spouse Owned badge
  • Eligible for the weekly “What's Happening” email to local families
  • Families can save you to their Favorites and turn on alerts

Go live with Beacon Beta perk · 3 a month

  • Broadcast “open now” and your live location the moment you open — built for food trucks, pop-ups, and home-based vendors
  • Live pin for mobile vendors; open-now signal for home-based businesses (your address stays protected)
  • Lights up your listing, hits the live feed and the map, and notifies the families who follow you
Founding businesses: our paid plan (Promote) is paused for now, so your listing is free during the beta — and Beacon is included as a perk, 3 a month, so you can put it to the test and help us shape it before it becomes a paid feature.

Manage it yourself

  • Claim and edit anytime — changes go live instantly
  • A “Last updated” freshness mark on your listing
  • See your views and saves

Coming soon

  • Promote (paid): more Beacons — up to 5 a week — plus push deals & specials, guaranteed newsletter inclusion, and basic analytics
  • Featured (later): unlimited Beacons, priority placement in search and “near you,” advanced analytics, and multi-location
  • Beacon enhancements: schedule a Beacon in advance, and “nearby” alerts that reach your followers when you're close
