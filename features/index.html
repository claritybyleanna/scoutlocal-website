Belong faster after every move.

ScoutLocal is the trusted local guide for military families — the businesses and places worth knowing near your base, plus the military-spouse entrepreneurs worth supporting anywhere. Free for families. A home for the businesses who serve them.

The signature feature

Go live with Beacon.

Some of the best local businesses don't keep fixed hours — food trucks, pop-ups, home-based makers. Beacon lets them broadcast “open now” and their live location the moment they open, and tells the families who follow them exactly where to go.

For families

See who's open right now on the map, with a live pin and a window that tells you how long they'll be there.

For businesses

One tap lights up your listing, hits the live feed, and notifies your followers. Home addresses stay protected.
