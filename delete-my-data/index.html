Delete my data

Your data, your call.

You can delete your ScoutLocal account and the personal data associated with it at any time. Here is how, what is removed, and how long it takes.

Email a deletion request

Last updated: June 9, 2026

ScoutLocal is a product of Novalyse LLC. This page explains how to delete your account and personal data from ScoutLocal.

Delete your account in the app

Open the ScoutLocal app, go to the Profile tab, open account settings, and choose Delete account. You will be asked to confirm. This permanently removes your account and associated personal data.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email support@novalyse.com from the email address on your account with the subject "Delete my ScoutLocal data." We may ask you to verify your identity before processing the request.

What gets deleted

Your account, profile and onboarding details, saved favorites, event suggestions, reports, feedback, push token, and other personal data tied to your account are deleted.

What we may keep

We may retain limited records where required for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes — for example, business subscription payment records held by our payment processor — and de-identified or aggregated information that can no longer be linked to you. Business listing information that exists independently of your personal account (for example, a publicly listed local business) may remain in the directory.

How long it takes

In-app deletion is immediate for your account. Any remaining copies in backups or connected services are removed within 30 days. We will confirm by email when an emailed request is complete.

Contact

Questions about deletion can be sent to support@novalyse.com.
