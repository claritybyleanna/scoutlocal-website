ADD YOUR BUSINESS PHOTOS HERE
=============================

The businesses page (businesses/index.html) shows a 3-photo strip that
expects these three files in this folder (docs/assets/img/):

  salon.jpg     -> the hair stylist / salon photo
  florist.jpg   -> the flower-shop owner photo
  computer.jpg  -> the person working at a computer (home-based / freelance)

Save (or rename) your three photos to exactly those names and drop them
in this folder. Until they're here, those image slots will appear empty.

Tip: big photos slow the page down. If a file is over ~500 KB, resize the
long edge to about 1200 px before adding it (or ask Claude to optimize them
once they're in the folder).
