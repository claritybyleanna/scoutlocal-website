04 / how the quest works

arrive, walk, stamp, win.

01

Check in & grab a map

Stop by the registration table, pick up your stamp map for $5 (cash or card), and sign the sign-in sheet.

Roam in any order

Hit the booths in any order — there's no set route. Say hi to each local business and see what they make.

Each business has its own stamp color. Fill in all sixteen circles to complete your map.

Turn in your completed map at the Scout Local table for a prize, grab swag, and you're in for the door prize.
