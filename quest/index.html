04 / how the quest works

arrive, walk, stamp, win.

01

Check in & grab a map

Stop by the registration table, pick up your stamp map for $5 (cash or card), and sign the sign-in sheet.

02

Roam in any order

Visit the booths in any order, collect a stamp from each listed business, and keep your map with you as you go.

03

Collect every stamp

Each business has its own stamp color. Fill every listed business circle to complete the Quest.

04

Claim your prize

Turn in your completed map at the Scout Local table for a prize, grab swag, and you're in for the door prize.
