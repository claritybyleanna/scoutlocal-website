Trust & promise

Trust should be easy to see. Not buried in guesswork.

Families should not have to guess whether a listing is real, current, or relevant. Businesses should not have to buy their way to the top.

See how it works
01 / the promise

Three things we stand for. In that order.

Trust

Every claim is verified or visibly unverified. Payment buys a listing, never position.

Freshness

Information is current or clearly marked as not current. Meaningful updates matter.

Belonging

ScoutLocal helps families rebuild a sense of home faster after every move.

02 / the never list

Things we will not do, even when it would be easier.

01

Sell badges or rank

02

Let owners hide reports

03

Auto-renew without seven days notice

04

Use dark patterns to discourage cancellation

05

Sell or share military family data with advertisers

06

Send notifications without a clear benefit

07

Use urgency, FOMO, or scarcity

08

Hide verified-closed status

09

Use sponsored slots without documented criteria
